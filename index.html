Who We Are

Born from John and Noah’s frustration with Kenya’s exploitative tourism, our startup crafts affordable, wildlife-first safaris that rewrites the rules. We partner with Maasai guides and conservancies to deliver uncrowded Big 5 encounters, while funneling profits into anti-poaching patrols and community lodges-because real adventure should protect the wild spaces it celebrates

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Kenya for a safari?

The best time for a safari in Kenya is during the dry season, from June to October, when wildlife is more active and visible.

Do I need a visa to visit Kenya?

Yes, most visitors require a visa to enter Kenya. You can apply online or upon arrival at the airport.

What should I pack for a safari in Kenya?

Pack lightweight, breathable clothing, comfortable shoes, a hat, sunscreen, insect repellent, and binoculars for wildlife viewing.
